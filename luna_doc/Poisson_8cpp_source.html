5 /// \f$ (x,y) \in [-1,1] \times [-1,1] \f$ subject to the boundary conditions
6 /// \f$ u(-1,y) = e^{-1} \sin(y) + y^2 \f$, \f$ u(1,y) = e \sin(y) + y^2 \f$,
7 /// \f$ u(x,-1) = e^x \sin(-1) + x^2 \f$ and \f$ u(x,1) = e^x \sin(1) + x^2 \f$.
8 /// The system is solved using a Chebyshev spectral method such that
9 /// \f[ u(x,y) \approx u_{IJ}(x,y) = \sum_{N=0}^{IJ-1} a_N \Phi_N(x,y) .\f]
10 /// Here the basis functions \f$ \Phi_N(x,y) = T_i(x) T_j(y) \f$ are a product
11 /// of one-dimensional Chebyshev basis functions with \f$i = 0,1,\ldots,I-1 \f$
12 /// and \f$ j=0,1,\ldots, J-1\f$ where \f$ I \f$ and \f$ J \f$ are the number of
13 /// collocation points in the \f$ x \f$ and \f$ y \f$ direction respectively.
14 /// The exact solution is given by \f[ u(x,y) = e^x \sin(y) + x^2 y^2. \f]
