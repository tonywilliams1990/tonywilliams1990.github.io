Detailed Description

A class for defining Spectral solutions using specified basis functions and coefficients.

The spectral function \( u_N(x) \) is defined by

\[ u_N(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{N} a_n \phi_n(x), \]

where \( a_n \) are coefficients and \( \phi_n(x) \) are known basis functions.
