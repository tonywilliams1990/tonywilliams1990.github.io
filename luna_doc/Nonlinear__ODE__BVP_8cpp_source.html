1 /// \file Nonlinear_ODE_BVP.cpp
2 /// \ingroup Examples
3 /// The equation \f[ (f(x)-1)f''(x) + \left( \frac{2}{x^2} - 1 \right)f'(x)^2
4 /// = 1 \f] is solved subject to the boundary conditions \f$ f(0) = 2 \f$ and
5 /// \f$ f \left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \right) = \frac{3}{2}. \f$ The system is
6 /// solved as a set of first-order equations in the form \f[ \begin{pmatrix}
7 /// 1 & 0 \\ 0 & f(x) - 1 \end{pmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} f(x) \\ f'(x)
8 /// \end{bmatrix}' = \begin{bmatrix} f'(x) \\ 1 + \left(1 - \frac{2}{x^2}
9 /// \right)f'(x)^2 \end{bmatrix}, \f]
10 /// The exact solution is given by \f$ f(x) = 1 + \sqrt{1-x^2}. \f$
