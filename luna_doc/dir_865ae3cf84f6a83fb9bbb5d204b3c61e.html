 The Falkner-Skan equation

\[ f'''(\eta) + f(\eta)f''(\eta) + \beta \left[1 - \left(f'(\eta) \right)^2 \right] = 0 \]

\[ u''(x) - (x^6 + 3x^2)u(x) = 0 \]

file  ODE_BVP_System.cpp [code]
 The system of equations

\[ u''(x) - u(x) + 2 v(x) = 2 \left(1 - 2 x e^{-x} \right), \]

\[ v''(x) + x^2 v(x) + u(x) = x^2 - e^{-x}, \]

is solved subject to the boundary conditions \( u(0) = v(0) = 0 \) and \( u(x) \to 0 \), \( v(x) \to 1 \) as \( x \to \infty \).

 
file  ODE_Rational.cpp [code]
 The equation

\[ u''(y) - u(y) = -2e^{1-y} \]

is solved on \( y \in [0,\infty)\) subject to the boundary conditions \( u(0) = e\) and \( u(y) \to 0 \) as \( y \to \infty\).

 
file  Poisson.cpp [code]
 The equation

\[ \nabla^2 u(x,y) = 2(x^2 + y^2) \]

is solved on the domain \( (x,y) \in [-1,1] \times [-1,1] \) subject to the boundary conditions \( u(-1,y) = e^{-1} \sin(y) + y^2 \), \( u(1,y) = e \sin(y) + y^2 \), \( u(x,-1) = e^x \sin(-1) + x^2 \) and \( u(x,1) = e^x \sin(1) + x^2 \).
