4 /// The system of equations \f[ u''(x) - u(x) + 2 v(x) = 2 \left(1 - 2 x e^{-x}
5 /// \right), \f] \f[ v''(x) + x^2 v(x) + u(x) = x^2 - e^{-x}, \f] is solved
6 /// subject to the boundary conditions \f$ u(0) = v(0) = 0 \f$ and \f$ u(x) \to
7 /// 0 \f$, \f$ v(x) \to 1 \f$ as \f$ x \to \infty \f$. The system is solved
8 /// using a rational Chebyshev spectral method such that \f[ u(x) \approx u_n(x)
9 /// = \sum_{i=0}^n a_i TL_{i}(x; L), \f] \f[ v(x) \approx v_n(x) = \sum_{i=0}^n
10 /// a_{n + 1 + i} TL_{i}(x; L). \f]
11 /// Here \f$ L \f$ is the map parameter associated with the rational Chebyshev
12 /// functions \f$ TL_i(y; L) \f$. The exact solution is given by \f$ u(x) = x^2
13 /// e^{-x} \f$ and \f$ v(x) = 1 - e^{-x} \f$.
